Matisse Grigoryan

I am a web design and developer professional. I graduated from Clackamas Community College with an AAS Web Design and Development. Working for 14 years in the interior design industry has inspired me on how to use balance and whitespace in design. Using my creativity and instinct for working with people is the backbone of working with my clients. My experience with sales and management allows me to use my skills for organization and client services to help achieve successful projects. I want to work with clients to create something they can be proud of and that can grow as they grow in their business model. I work in the Specialty Foods industry! This exposure to Consumer Package Goods is an education in branding, supporting product launches, and how to design a story brand.

When I am not working, I am traveling or enjoying nature with my son Michael! We love to explore – creativity, knowledge, and nature. We love being outside on nature walks and long road trips. I love food, eating, and living a clean lifestyle. I believe in cultivating community and cherish my family and friends. I feel blessed to be God’s child and to receive grace and love new every day!
