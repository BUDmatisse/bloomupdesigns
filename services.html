Branding

Branding is personal, personality, and purpose.

Your brand is an important part of the interaction and relationship with your client. I work with you to design and develop simple, clean design work. I keep the design balanced and allow whitespace to showcase your products or services. My branding approach is not to fill a page with your choice colors or fonts but bring alive supportive composition with balanced design. I will work closely with you, and most importantly listen to you.

Web Design

Web design is bringing your business brand to a global audience.

Your website needs to present your brand, showcase your product and/or services but more importantly solve your client needs. Why is someone visiting your website? In the web design process, I research your market's target audience, help determine their needs/wants, and design with those in mind. Once we agreed on a practical design, then the fun part! I use your logo, branding, and content to design all the visual candy.

Development

Web development is building the website from the design.

During this last phase of the project, I will work with the approved design to build a functional website. Your website should be easy to navigate and have your client's needs in mind. My web development is purposefully kept simple and direct. We want your client to get what they need without getting lost and losing interest. We will work on a couple of stages for approval before the final design stage.
